BitAlert

With the increase interest in cryptocurrencies the price of bitcoin skyrocketed. I could not keep an eye on it every minute of every day and I wanted to build a reliable alert system. BitAlert allows users to create daily alerts to be notified via sms or email. Multiple cron jobs run every minute of every day on a rasberry pi server to monitor prices and send out messages.
